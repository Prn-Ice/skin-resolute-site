Identifier · 5 skin types

Identify your skin type.

Five honest profiles, written the way she talks. Dry, oily, combination, sensitive, or normal. Read the one that sounds like your face, then meet it where it actually is.

Not sure? Try the 30-minute Bare-Face test
D
Hydration

Dry

Tight after cleansing, flaky patches and a dull, tired look. Your skin isn't making enough natural oil, so it loses water fast and the barrier stays thirsty.

Signs
  • Tightness after washing
  • Flaky, rough patches
  • Dullness & visible fine lines
It craves

A rich moisturiser, humectants like glycerin & hyaluronic acid, a nourishing oil, and softer, lukewarm water.

Feed it well, my love. It just thirsty.
O
Sebum Control

Oily

Shiny all over, especially the T-zone, with bigger pores and surprise breakouts. Your glands simply make extra sebum (your natural oil), and if you strip it too hard it fights back with even more.

Signs
  • Midday shine all over
  • Enlarged, visible pores
  • Frequent breakouts
It craves

A gentle gel cleanser, niacinamide, a light non-comedogenic moisturiser (yes, still moisturise!) and daily SPF.

No strip am dry, abeg. That one na trap.
C
Dual Action

Combination

An oily T-zone living next to normal-or-dry cheeks. It is basically two skin types on one face: the middle has more oil glands, so it shines while the sides stay drier.

Signs
  • Shiny forehead, nose & chin
  • Normal-to-dry cheeks
  • Bigger pores in the centre
It craves

Hydrate the dry cheeks well, and keep the oily T-zone light and balanced. Two zones, two habits.

Balance am gently and your glow go steady.
S
Calming Care

Sensitive

Redness, itching or stinging that flares the moment the weather turns or a new product says hello. Your barrier, the skin's protective wall, is delicate and reacts before it adjusts.

Signs
  • Redness & warmth
  • Stings with new products
  • Reacts to weather & fragrance
It craves

Minimal, fragrance-free formulas, calming ceramides and oat or centella, and patch-test everything first.

Gentle, gentle, my dear. Less is more.
N
Daily Glow

Normal

Balanced and comfortable, with small pores, rare breakouts and an even glow. Your oil and water sit in happy balance, so the job is simply to protect what you already have.

Signs
  • Comfortable, never tight
  • Small, even pores
  • Breakouts are rare
It craves

Consistent gentle care, antioxidants and daily SPF to protect that lovely balance.

Hold am steady. Consistency over miracles.
