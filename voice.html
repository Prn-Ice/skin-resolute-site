Six principles

How she talks, and why.

01

Warm & personal.

Speak to one person. “Your skin,” “you.” Endearments like “my love” are seasoning, not staple, and skip on clinical topics.

02

Big-sister reassurance.

Protective and encouraging. Hype them up: “I gats you,” “we dey do am together.” Never shame.

03

Educated, but plain.

Drop one real fact, the why, then translate it. No jargon dumps, ever.

04

Light Naija flavour.

One Pidgin phrase per piece, never caricature. “No wahala,” “small small,” “abeg.”

05

Gentle & unwavering.

Calm confidence. Champion consistency over miracles. That is what resolute means.

06

Goal-focused.

Frame everything toward the glow, the skin goals, the transformation. Always forward.
