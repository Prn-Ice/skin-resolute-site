The Bare-Face
Method.

The honest 30-minute test that tells you what your skin actually needs. No products, no filters, no guessing. Just you, a clean face, and natural light.

What you need
30min
  • A gentle, sulphate-free cleanser
  • A clean, soft towel
  • No products for 30 min
  • Natural daylight
01
Cleanse

Wash your face.

Start with a clean face. Use a gentle, sulphate-free cleanser to lift off makeup, oil and the day's dirt, so your skin is fresh and neutral. Nothing harsh.

♡

Gentle, my love. Harsh soap go strip your skin and spoil the test.

, the rule
02
Pat Dry

Pat dry, don't rub.

Press your skin softly with a clean, soft towel. Rubbing tugs at the barrier and can wake up redness, so be tender with it.

♡

Pat am soft small, like you dey handle silk.

, the touch
03
Wait 30 min

Wait thirty minutes.

No products, no touching. Give your skin half an hour to settle back to its natural state, so what you see next is the real, honest result, not what the cleanser left behind.

♡

Hands off, abeg! Let your skin breathe and show you the truth.

, the patience
04
Observe

Observe in good light.

Look in natural light. Read your face honestly, then read it kindly:

Tight / flaky means dry
Shiny all over means oily
T-zone shine only means combination
Comfortable / even means normal
Red / itchy means sensitive, patch-test everything next
♡

Now look well, sis. Your skin just told you exactly what it needs.
